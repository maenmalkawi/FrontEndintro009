Lionel Andrés Messi

born 24 June 1987), also known as Leo Messi, is an Argentine professional footballer who plays as a forward for and captains both Major League Soccer club Inter Miami and the Argentina national team. Widely regarded as one of the greatest players of all time

Until leaving the club in 2021, he had spent his entire professional career with Barcelona, where he won a club-record 34 trophies, including ten La Liga titles, seven Copa del Rey titles and the UEFA Champions League four times. With his country, he won the 2021 Copa América and the 2022 FIFA World Cup.

Messi's trophy cabinet at the international level has quickly filled over the past two years with the 2022 World Cup proving his crowning glory. The Argentine broke his senior international trophy drought in 2021 as he won the Copa America final at his fourth attempt. Prior to that, Messi had won titles with Argentina's U20 and Olympic team over a decade earlier.

He is named THE GOAT after he won all possible championships in the world of football
